# Text To Video

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/workflow-rules.ts`

## Definition

The user starts from a written brief and needs the strategist to choose a video model directly.

## Best For

Cinematic scenes, character action, social concepts, and rapid creative exploration.

## Avoid For

Exact product packshots without a reference image and brand assets that require strict logo reproduction.

## Recommended Prompt Structures

cinematic-scene, social-ad, character-scene

## Planning Steps

- Identify the use case and output format.
- Choose the tier by quality, cost, and speed pressure.
- Convert the brief into a single coherent scene prompt.

## Tier Guidance

- Best: premium realism, human emotion, or brand-film quality.
- Medium: strong output with practical iteration speed.
- Value: exploration, social variants, and early motion tests.
