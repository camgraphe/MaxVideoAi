# Veo 3.1

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Best
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, text-to-image-then-image-to-video, video-to-video
- Relative cost: premium
- Relative speed: slow
- Quality level: premium
- Scores: realism 5, motion 5, product detail 4, social ad 4

## Best For

Cinematic realism, premium brand films, human emotion, natural camera movement, and audio-ready 4K Veo outputs.

## Avoid For

Low-cost ad variant batches, exact packaging text, and rapid throwaway drafts.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: top realism, natural motion, cinematic lighting, strong human-scene credibility, and native audio with 4K option where exposed.

Weaknesses: premium relative cost and less direct product-ad specialization than Kling 4K.

## Prompt Guidance

- Describe the human or physical motivation behind the action.
- Use realistic camera and lighting details that support the scene.
- Prefer one coherent cinematic moment over a list of visual effects.
- Use concise ambience, SFX, or dialogue cues when native audio or lip-sync matters.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid exact text, label, or UI reproduction requests.
- Avoid mixing multiple genre styles in one short prompt.
