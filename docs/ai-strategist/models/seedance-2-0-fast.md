# Seedance 2.0 Fast

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Value
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, text-to-image-then-image-to-video
- Relative cost: low
- Relative speed: very-fast
- Quality level: standard
- Scores: realism 4, motion 4, product detail 3, social ad 4

## Best For

Fast iteration loops, paid social variants, timing tests, and motion-first drafts.

## Avoid For

Final hero product ads, precise packaging text, 4K product detail, and high-control brand films.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: very fast draft speed, good motion energy, and strong value for testing multiple concepts.

Weaknesses: less fine detail than premium models and lower final polish for product closeups.

## Prompt Guidance

- State the main subject and action in the first sentence.
- Use one camera move and one lighting direction instead of stacking many controls.
- Keep product claims simple and visual for fast A/B tests.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid exact small text or labels.
- Avoid dense multi-shot continuity requirements in a single prompt.
