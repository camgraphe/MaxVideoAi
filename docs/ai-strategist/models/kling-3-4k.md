# Kling 3 4K

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Best
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, text-to-image-then-image-to-video
- Relative cost: premium
- Relative speed: slow
- Quality level: premium
- Scores: realism 5, motion 4, product detail 5, social ad 4

## Best For

Premium product hero videos, 4K brand assets, ecommerce detail, and high-end social ads.

## Avoid For

Low-budget exploration, throwaway meme variants, and very fast timing tests.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: excellent product detail, native 4K output, premium finish, high-resolution brand polish, and native audio where available.

Weaknesses: premium relative cost and slower iteration than value models.

## Prompt Guidance

- Describe materials, finish, reflections, and scale with concrete nouns.
- Use a simple camera move that protects product readability.
- Include end-frame composition for brand and ecommerce uses.
- Use subtle ambience or SFX for product reveals unless the approval pass should stay silent.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid cluttered prop styling that hides the product.
- Avoid asking for unreadably small typography or legal copy.
