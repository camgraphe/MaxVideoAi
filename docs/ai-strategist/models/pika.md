# Pika

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Value
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video
- Relative cost: low
- Relative speed: fast
- Quality level: standard
- Scores: realism 3, motion 3, product detail 2, social ad 4

## Best For

Playful social videos, stylized short clips, creator experiments, and lightweight visual concepts.

## Avoid For

Strict photorealism, premium product assets, and complex camera continuity.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: accessible creative range, good short-form energy, and useful playful brand moments.

Weaknesses: lower realism and product-detail ceiling with less predictability for premium commercial work.

## Prompt Guidance

- Use it for quick social hooks and stylized motion concepts.
- Keep transformations simple and visually legible.
- Specify the short-form context when relevant.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid exact product, logo, or typography reproduction.
- Avoid asking for realistic multi-person continuity.
