# Kling 3 Standard

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Value
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, text-to-image-then-image-to-video
- Relative cost: low
- Relative speed: fast
- Quality level: high
- Scores: realism 4, motion 4, product detail 4, social ad 5

## Best For

Value product ads, vertical social creative, creator-style demos, and commercial motion tests.

## Avoid For

Premium 4K packshots, complex cinematic storytelling, and strict brand asset recreation.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: good ad-readiness for cost, solid product motion, fast enough variant testing, and native audio/lip-sync workflows where available.

Weaknesses: less detail control than Kling Pro or 4K and less premium finish on hero assets.

## Prompt Guidance

- Lead with the product and intended ad format.
- Specify the surface, reflections, and camera move for product clarity.
- Use concise social platform language such as hook, reveal, and loop.
- Keep dialogue short when using audio or lip-sync workflows.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid premium claims that depend on tiny label accuracy.
- Avoid overloading the shot with many secondary props.
