# MiniMax Hailuo 02 Standard

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Value
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video
- Relative cost: low
- Relative speed: fast
- Quality level: standard
- Scores: realism 3, motion 3, product detail 2, social ad 3

## Best For

Budget motion tests, low-stakes creative drafts, playful social experiments, and silent motion concepts.

## Avoid For

Premium commercial recommendations, serious product detail, strict brand assets, and hero-quality realism.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: budget-friendly testing, quick concept coverage, and experimental social motion.

Weaknesses: lower premium ceiling than Kling, Veo, Seedance, or LTX and less reliable serious commercial detail.

## Prompt Guidance

- Use it for low-stakes creative testing before committing to a main commercial model.
- Keep the scene simple and motion-led.
- Treat the output as an exploratory direction, not final brand production.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid premium product fidelity expectations.
- Avoid exact logos, small text, or complex character continuity.

## Routing Notes

Rank below Kling, Veo, Seedance, and LTX for serious commercial or premium recommendations.
