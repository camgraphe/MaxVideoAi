# Veo 3.1 Fast

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Medium
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, text-to-image-then-image-to-video, video-to-video
- Relative cost: high
- Relative speed: fast
- Quality level: high
- Scores: realism 5, motion 4, product detail 4, social ad 4

## Best For

Fast realistic previews, cinematic social drafts, client review options, natural motion scenes, and 4K-capable Veo tests.

## Avoid For

Lowest-cost volume testing, strict packaging ecommerce closeups, and strict logo reconstruction.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: strong realism at better speed, good cinematic framing, natural motion feel, and native audio with 4K option where exposed.

Weaknesses: less premium than full Veo 3.1 and less product-detail focused than Kling Pro.

## Prompt Guidance

- Ground the scene in observable physical action.
- Use natural camera and lighting language instead of abstract style stacks.
- Keep the prompt focused on one emotional or visual beat.
- Add ambience, SFX, or a short spoken line when audio or lip-sync is part of the brief.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid treating the fast model as the final product-detail path.
- Avoid asking for several unrelated shots at once.
