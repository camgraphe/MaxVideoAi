# Kling 3 Pro

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Medium
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, text-to-image-then-image-to-video
- Relative cost: high
- Relative speed: medium
- Quality level: high
- Scores: realism 4, motion 4, product detail 5, social ad 5

## Best For

Product ads, brand social campaigns, controlled camera moves, and commercial hero drafts.

## Avoid For

Lowest-cost bulk tests and long narrative scenes with many emotional beats.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: strong product detail, commercial polish, reliable social ad composition, and native audio/lip-sync workflows where available.

Weaknesses: higher cost than value models and not as cinematic as Veo for human drama.

## Prompt Guidance

- Name the hero product and its material details early.
- Describe the camera move as one clean commercial reveal.
- Use brand-safe words for lighting, color, and background.
- Add concise ambience, SFX, or short dialogue when the commercial needs audio.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid asking for too many scene changes in one output.
- Avoid contradictory surfaces, lighting directions, or camera paths.
