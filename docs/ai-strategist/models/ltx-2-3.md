# LTX 2.3

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Value
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, video-to-video
- Relative cost: low
- Relative speed: very-fast
- Quality level: standard
- Scores: realism 3, motion 4, product detail 3, social ad 4

## Best For

Prompt testing, low-cost drafts, vertical social exploration, animatics, and audio-ready storyboard timing.

## Avoid For

Photoreal final heroes, fine product detail, and premium cinematic realism.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: very fast exploration, low relative cost, good prompt-direction learning, and useful native-audio timing for drafts.

Weaknesses: lower realism ceiling and weaker product fidelity than Kling or Veo.

## Prompt Guidance

- Use it to test the idea, timing, and visual language before a premium render.
- Keep prompts short and revise one variable at a time.
- State vertical, square, or landscape framing explicitly.
- Include simple ambience, SFX, or a short dialogue cue when audio/lip-sync timing matters.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid relying on it for final photoreal product detail.
- Avoid long prompts with many nested constraints.
