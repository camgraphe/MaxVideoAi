# Sora

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Best
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, video-to-video
- Relative cost: premium
- Relative speed: medium
- Quality level: premium
- Scores: realism 5, motion 5, product detail 3, social ad 4

## Best For

Narrative scenes, imaginative concepts, complex prompt following, and premium cinematic ideas.

## Avoid For

Lowest-cost production, exact product packaging, and strict ecommerce packshots.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: strong scene coherence, high creative range, and premium story and motion quality.

Weaknesses: premium relative cost and less focus on exact product detail than Kling 4K.

## Prompt Guidance

- Give the model a coherent scene arc and ending frame.
- Use vivid but non-conflicting visual details.
- Reserve it for ideas where story and continuity matter.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid exact label, logo, or small text requirements.
- Avoid using it as the cheapest path for simple variant testing.
