# Veo 3.1 Lite

Source of truth: `frontend/lib/ai-strategist/model-catalog.ts`

## Strategic Position

- Tier: Value
- Workflows: text-to-video, image-to-video, text-to-image-then-image-to-video, video-to-video
- Relative cost: low
- Relative speed: fast
- Quality level: standard
- Scores: realism 4, motion 4, product detail 3, social ad 4

## Best For

Budget Veo drafts, low-cost realistic tests, audio-ready social previews, and fast cinematic iteration.

## Avoid For

4K delivery, premium final hero shots, strict product preservation, complex reference-to-video control, and general source-video restyling outside extend workflows.

## Strengths And Weaknesses

Strengths: very low Veo-family cost, realistic motion language, native audio, and good value for budget previews.

Weaknesses: no 4K output in the Lite route, lower fidelity than Veo 3.1 or Veo 3.1 Fast, and less product-detail control than Kling.

## Prompt Guidance

- Use it when the brief wants a budget Veo-family pass before a higher-fidelity render.
- Keep the action simple and visible within 4-8 seconds.
- Use native audio for ambience, SFX, or one short line when timing matters.
- Treat source-video usage as an extend workflow, not a general video restyle route.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

- Avoid 4K delivery expectations on Lite.
- Avoid strict packaging, logo, or tiny text preservation.
