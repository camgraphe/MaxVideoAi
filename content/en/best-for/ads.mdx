## What this page is for
Ad generation is different from UGC. A paid ad needs a clear product, controlled framing, reliable brand mood, fast variant testing, and a final clip that can survive review. The best AI video generator for ads is the model that lets you move from concept to usable creative without losing the offer, product, or visual system.

## Best picks
1. **[Seedance 2.0](/models/seedance-2-0)** - best for cinematic ad motion, native audio, reference-guided variants, and stronger visual quality.
2. **[Kling 3 Pro](/models/kling-3-pro)** - best for storyboarded ads, multi-shot sequences, product reveals, and controlled scene planning.
3. **[Veo 3.1](/models/veo-3-1)** - best for polished ad-ready shots, product scenes, and fast creative review.
4. **[Happy Horse 1.0](/models/happy-horse-1-0)** - best when ad concepts mainly need a unified text, image, reference, V2V, native audio, and lip-sync workflow.

## Why these models rank here
Seedance 2.0 is the strongest ad model for campaign iteration: one product direction, multiple hooks, multiple visual treatments, and reference-guided variants. It is a better first pick than Happy Horse when image quality, realism, and movement decide whether the ad is usable.

Kling 3 Pro should be used when the ad needs structure: opening shot, product reveal, benefit demonstration, closing frame. Its multi-prompt and Elements features make it a strong pick for short commercial sequences.

Veo 3.1 is a strong paid-social engine when you need a single polished shot quickly. It is especially practical for product-in-context visuals, lifestyle inserts, and simple offer-led video.

Happy Horse 1.0 remains useful for ad teams that need more than one input mode in a single model. It can start from text, animate an approved image, use R2V references, or edit an existing/generated clip while keeping native audio and lip-sync available for social and UGC-ad variants.

## What to use for each ad format

## FAQ
### What is the best AI video generator for ads?
Seedance 2.0 is the best all-around starting point for ads because it is stronger on visual quality, motion, and reference-led variants. Kling 3 Pro is strongest for storyboarded sequences, Veo 3.1 for polished shots, and Happy Horse 1.0 for unified reference/audio/edit workflows.

### Should I use UGC models for paid ads?
Only if the ad format is creator-led. For product demos, ecommerce, and brand spots, use an ad-focused workflow instead.

### Which model is best for product ads?
Use Seedance 2.0, Kling 3 Pro, or Veo 3.1 for structured product storytelling and reference-guided variants.
