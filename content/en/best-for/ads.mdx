## What this page is for
Ad generation is different from UGC. A paid ad needs a clear product, controlled framing, reliable brand mood, fast variant testing, and a final clip that can survive review. The best AI video generator for ads is the model that lets you move from concept to usable creative without losing the offer, product, or visual system.

## Best picks
1. **[Seedance 2.0](/models/seedance-2-0)** - best overall for ad concepts that need cinematic motion, native audio, and reference-guided variants.
2. **[Veo 3.1](/models/veo-3-1)** - best for polished ad-ready shots, product scenes, and fast creative review.
3. **[Kling 3 Pro](/models/kling-3-pro)** - best for storyboarded ads, multi-shot sequences, product reveals, and controlled scene planning.
4. **[LTX 2.3 Pro](/models/ltx-2-3-pro)** - best when production workflow matters: generate, extend, retake, audio-led motion, and 4K final options.

## Why these models rank here
Seedance 2.0 is the strongest default for ad teams that need more than one clean shot. It can take text, image, and reference material, which makes it useful for campaign iteration: one product direction, multiple hooks, multiple visual treatments.

Veo 3.1 is a strong paid-social engine when you need a single polished shot quickly. It is especially practical for product-in-context visuals, lifestyle inserts, and simple offer-led video.

Kling 3 Pro should be used when the ad needs structure: opening shot, product reveal, benefit demonstration, closing frame. Its multi-prompt and Elements features make it a strong pick for short commercial sequences.

LTX 2.3 Pro is valuable when the first render is not the final workflow. If you expect to extend the winning shot, retake a section, use audio-to-video, or deliver higher resolution, LTX can reduce production friction.

## What to use for each ad format
- **Product hero ad:** Seedance 2.0, Kling 3 Pro, LTX 2.3 Pro.
- **Lifestyle paid social:** Veo 3.1, Seedance 2.0.
- **Storyboarded mini-ad:** Kling 3 Pro, Seedance 2.0, Sora 2 Pro.
- **4K delivery:** Kling 3 4K or LTX 2.3 Pro.
- **Creator-style ad:** see [Best AI video generator for UGC videos](/ai-video-engines/best-for/ugc-ads).

## FAQ
### What is the best AI video generator for ads?
Seedance 2.0 is the best all-around starting point, Veo 3.1 is strong for polished ad shots, Kling 3 Pro is best for storyboarded ad sequences, and LTX 2.3 Pro is best for production workflows.

### Should I use UGC models for paid ads?
Only if the ad format is creator-led. For product demos, ecommerce, and brand spots, use an ad-focused workflow instead.

### Which model is best for product ads?
Use Seedance 2.0 or Kling 3 Pro for structured product storytelling, and LTX 2.3 Pro when you need retake, extend, or high-resolution final output.
