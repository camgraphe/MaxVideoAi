## What you're optimizing for
Image-to-video needs strong preservation of the source image with subtle motion. Consistency and control matter more than raw speed.

## Top picks (V1)
1. **Sora 2** - high fidelity with controlled motion.
2. **Veo 3.1** - balanced quality and speed.
3. **Kling 2.6 Pro** - cinematic motion with stylistic control.

## How we rank
We prioritize consistency, fidelity, and motion realism, then speed and cost.
