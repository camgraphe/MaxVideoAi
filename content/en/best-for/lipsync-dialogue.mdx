## What you're optimizing for
Dialogue-driven scenes need stable faces, accurate mouth shapes, and reliable timing. Audio support and low failure rates matter more than raw motion.

## Top picks (V1)
1. **Sora 2** - premium sync and fidelity.
2. **Veo 3.1** - strong balance of speed and quality.
3. **Kling 2.6 Pro** - cinematic output with controlled motion.

## How we rank
We prioritize lipsync quality, facial consistency, and overall fidelity, then speed and cost.
