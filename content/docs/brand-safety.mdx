MaxVideo AI layers automatic filters across prompts, reference media, and rendered outputs. Sensitive keywords trigger human review with audit trails.

The goal is to keep production teams moving without hiding risk. Before a render starts, the workspace checks the prompt, uploaded references, and selected model family against policy signals that can affect advertising, customer-facing social posts, and internal approval workflows.

When a prompt is blocked or escalated, producers can adjust the wording, remove risky references, or ask an admin to review the request. This keeps brand guidance visible inside the same workflow where teams compare models, estimate cost, and prepare final video assets.

- Configure escalation rules from **Settings → Trust**.
- Request custom allowlists for campaign-specific language.
- Track every block and override through the Status page.
